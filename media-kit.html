Short bio

Alexandra Gulamova is a co-founder of Savant.chat, the AI-powered security auditor for any code, trusted by 1inch, Lido, BGD Labs and TON. She has spent 10+ years in business development for blockchain and IT companies and leads partnerships, enterprise accounts and investor relations at Savant.chat. She also leads business development at ZeroPool, a zero-knowledge privacy protocol. Alexandra graduated from the Faculty of Public Administration at Lomonosov Moscow State University, studied at Sciences Po Rennes on an exchange program, and is based in Dubai, UAE.
